Students' Portal:

If you would like to check your mark in a particular course, please follow the instructions below:

1. Please visit the following page: Mark for each course
2. Please enter your unique ID and name of the course for which you would like to check your mark, and then click the "Get mark" button. Your mark in this course shall appear on the screen.

If you would like to check the marks in all of your courses, please follow the instructions below:

1. Please visit the following page: Check all your Marks here
2.Please enter your unique ID, and then click the "Get marks" button. Your marks in all courses shall appear on the screen.
